Recipe Manager Project

Zest was a group project that was developed in our Software Engineering course at UCSD. We made a recipe manager app that pulled recipes from the Spoonacular API. These recipes included ingredients, steps, and any dietary restrictions. Users were also able to create their own recipes, which included the same amount of info.

To dive deeper into current-day practices, we developed our site under a CRUD model. Therefore, creating, reading, updating/editing, deleting recipes were features of our app.

We also had features that made the site more accesible to others. For one, there was text-to-speech that would activate when pressing the TTS button or any of the steps. There was also a unit conversion built into our app for a variety of demographics.
